### About me

I have worked with Istio since 2017, with my primary focus being advocacy, documentation, and governance. Since joining the steering committee in 2020, I have led many of the administrative functions of the project, including the transfer to, and [graduation within](https://istio.io/blog/2023/istio-graduates-within-cncf/), the CNCF. For the past two years, I have also served as the maintainer representative on the CNCF Governing Board.

In the past few years, my contributions have included:

- Leading docs maintainer ([#1 contributor](https://istio.devstats.cncf.io/d/66/developer-activity-counts-by-companies?orgId=1&var-period_name=Last%20year&var-metric=contributions&var-repogroup_name=istio%2Fistio.io&var-country_name=All&var-companies=All) to istio.io, #6 overall to the project)

- Guiding the project's messaging and market positioning as ambient mode reached GA

- Designing and administering the Steering and Technical Oversight Committee (TOC) election processes, ensuring fair, transparent, and community-driven project leadership

- Managing our community of vendors, coordinating event strategy, and mentoring new contributors

Other things I've done for Istio include designing its website, writing release announcements, supporting the creation of the GAMMA project, and chairing IstioCon. (See [here](https://elections.istio.io/app/elections/2022/candidates/craigbox) for a full list.)

### Why I'm running

I continue to enjoy being involved in administering the Istio project, and providing context and continuity within the leadership group.

My priority remains supporting the broad ecosystem: ensuring our governance runs smoothly, facilitating fair and neutral collaboration across our diverse community of corporate contributors, and maintaining the level playing field that makes Istio's open-source model so successful.

There are major opportunities and changes in our space with the deprecation of other gateway platforms, the adoption of agentic AI, and the continued growth of ambient mode. The quality of our documentation, the clarity of our messaging, and a smooth user journey are more critical than ever for driving top-of-funnel growth.

If elected, I am happy to continue contributing to the administrative, governance, and advocacy "glue work" so that our contributor community can focus on what they do best: building a world-class service mesh.
